How to play?

üéÆ How to Practice?

The game interface is divided into three main areas to help you practice effectively:

  • Movement Cards (Left): Browse and select from movement cards. Click any card to start practicing that movement.
  • Movement Demonstration (Top Left): Watch the animated stick figure perform the selected movement as your reference guide.
  • Detection Area (Right): Your live camera feed with real-time skeleton tracking. A "Fit" indicator shows how well you match the target pose (0-100%).

‚ú® Practice Flow:

  • Step 1: Select a movement card from the left panel.
  • Step 2: Follow the stick figure demonstration at the top.
  • Step 3: Perform the movement in front of the camera until Fit reaches 90%+ and hold for 1 second.
  • Step 4: Upon success, you'll see a celebration message! Switch to the next card to continue.
